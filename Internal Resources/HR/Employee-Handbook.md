## 2. EMPLOYMENT STATUS & CLASSIFICATIONS

### Full-Time Employees

Full-time employees work minimum 40 hours per week and are eligible for:
- Health insurance (medical, dental, vision)
- 401(k) retirement plan
- Paid time off (PTO)
- Professional development budget
- Company-paid software and tools

### Part-Time Employees

Part-time employees work less than 30 hours per week and are eligible for:
- Pro-rated PTO

Contractors are not employees and are not eligible for company benefits. Terms defined in contractor agreement.

## 4. TIME OFF & PAID TIME OFF (PTO)

### PTO Policy

Elevra offers generous PTO for work-life balance and personal well-being.

**Annual PTO Allocation:**
- **Full-time employees:** 20 days/year (160 hours)
  - 10 days vacation
  - 5 days sick leave
  - 5 days personal/flexible use
- **Part-time employees:** Pro-rated (10 + 2.5 + 2.5 days)

**Accrual:** PTO accrues each pay period. Unused PTO at year-end carries over up to 5 days (rest forfeited).

### Using PTO

**Request Process:**
1. Submit PTO request in [system] at least 2 weeks in advance
2. Manager approves based on project schedule
3. Confirm with team and client before taking time

**Short Notice:** For last-minute time off (illness, emergency), notify manager ASAP.

**Coverage:** Before taking PTO, ensure projects are covered and deliverables won't be impacted.

### Sick Leave

Sick leave is for:
- Personal illness or medical appointments
- Care for family member
- Bereavement
- Legal or medical matters

**Certification:** For absences over 3 consecutive days, medical certification may be required.

### Company Holidays

Elevra observes the following holidays:
- New Year's Day (January 1)
- Memorial Day (last Monday in May)
- Independence Day (July 4)
- Labor Day (first Monday in September)
- Thanksgiving (4th Thursday in November + Friday)
- Christmas (December 25)

**Holiday Closure:** If a holiday falls on a weekend, closure may shift to Friday or Monday.

**Remote Work:** If holiday is observed as remote day, no PTO is required.

### Unpaid Leave

Elevra may grant unpaid leave for:
- Extended personal matters
- Sabbaticals (discuss with management)
- Family/medical leave (FMLA may apply, see legal counsel)

**Approval:** Unpaid leave requires manager and Alexandria approval.

## 9. CONFIDENTIALITY & IP OWNERSHIP

### Confidential Information

You agree to protect Elevra's confidential information:
- Client information and strategies
- Pricing and fee structures
- Proprietary methodologies and frameworks
- Internal communications and financial data
- Passwords and system access
- Vendor and partner information

**Obligation:** Don't disclose confidential information to anyone outside Elevra without written permission.

**Duration:** Confidentiality continues indefinitely for trade secrets, 3 years post-employment for other information.

### Intellectual Property

All work you create for Elevra (designs, code, copy, strategy, etc.) is owned by Elevra. You assign all IP rights to Elevra upon creation.

**Work-for-Hire:** Your work is "work made for hire" under copyright law; Elevra owns it from creation.

**Personal Projects:** Projects created on your own time, with your own resources, unrelated to Elevra, are yours (unless they use Elevra methodologies or benefit Elevra).

### Confidentiality Agreement

## 10. DATA SECURITY & TECHNOLOGY

### Company Devices

Company-issued devices (laptop, phone, monitor, etc.) are Elevra property.

**Usage:** For work purposes only (personal use is permitted in off-hours if not conflicting with work)

**Security:**
- Use strong password (minimum 12 characters, mixed case/numbers/symbols)
- Lock screen when away from desk
- Don't download suspicious files
- Report any breaches or lost devices immediately

**Return:** Upon termination, return all devices in working condition.

### Passwords & Access

- **Change regularly:** Every 90 days or per IT instructions
- **Don't share:** Never share passwords; use password manager
- **Secure:** Use strong, unique passwords for each service
- **Report:** Report suspected password compromise immediately

### Data Protection

- **Client data:** Handle with confidentiality; don't store outside secure systems
- **Personal data:** Don't store unnecessary personal information
- **Encryption:** Use VPN for remote work; use HTTPS for sensitive data
- **Cloud storage:** Use company-approved cloud services (Google Drive, etc.) only

### BYOD (Bring Your Own Device)

If using personal device for work:
- Ensure it's secure and updated
- Use VPN for any work access
- Install company-approved security software
- Don't download non-work apps on work accounts
- Company reserves right to remote wipe if needed

## 15. DISCIPLINE & TERMINATION

### Disciplinary Process

**For minor violations:**
1. Verbal warning and discussion
2. Written warning if behavior continues
3. Final warning if no improvement
4. Termination for continued violation

**For serious violations:**
- Can result in immediate termination (no warnings)
- Examples: Dishonesty, theft, violence, breach of confidentiality, illegal activity

### Termination

Elevra is an at-will employer. Either party may terminate employment at any time, for any lawful reason, with or without cause.

**Notice:** Professional courtesy is 2 weeks' notice (not required, but appreciated).

**Final Paycheck:** All earned compensation, unused PTO (if applicable by law), and reimbursements paid on final check.

**Severance:** Severance is discretionary and not guaranteed.
