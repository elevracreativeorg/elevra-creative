# Master Service Agreement (MSA)

**Elevra Creative**
Houston, Texas

---

## 1. ENGAGEMENT & SERVICES

This Master Service Agreement ("**MSA**") establishes the terms under which **Elevra Creative, LLC** ("**Elevra**" or "**Service Provider**") provides creative, strategic, and digital services to the undersigned client ("**Client**").

Services are detailed in a Statement of Work ("**SOW**") executed concurrently with or following this MSA. Each SOW specifies deliverables, timeline, pricing, and success metrics for that engagement.

Elevra provides full-spectrum brand partnership services including:
- Brand architecture, positioning, and messaging strategy
- Visual identity and design systems
- Website development and digital strategy
- Content production (photography, video, editorial)
- Paid media and advertising optimization
- CRM and marketing automation implementation
- Ongoing strategic advisory and retainer partnerships

---

## 2. TERM & TERMINATION

**Term:** This MSA begins on the date signed and continues for one (1) year, unless extended or terminated earlier.

**Termination for Convenience:** Either party may terminate this MSA with thirty (30) days written notice to the other party. Termination does not relieve Client of payment obligations for completed work or services rendered through the termination date.

**Termination for Cause:** Either party may terminate immediately if the other party materially breaches this MSA and fails to cure the breach within fifteen (15) days of written notice.

**Effect of Termination:** Upon termination, Client remains obligated to pay invoices due. Elevra will deliver all completed work and transfer ownership of deliverables as specified in Section 5 (Intellectual Property).

---

## 3. FEES & PAYMENT TERMS

**Pricing Model:** Elevra operates on a retainer, project, or hybrid engagement basis, as specified in each SOW.

**Fee Structure:**
- Monthly retainers: invoiced on the first of each month; services begin on invoice date
- Project-based work: invoiced per SOW milestones (typically 50% at kickoff, 50% upon completion)
- Hourly work: billed in 0.5-hour increments at rates specified in SOW

**Invoices & Payment:**
- Invoices are due within thirty (30) days of receipt
- Payment method: ACH transfer, wire, or check to Elevra's designated account
- Late payments accrue 1.5% monthly interest (18% annual)
- All fees are non-refundable once services are rendered

**Expenses:**
- Out-of-pocket expenses (subcontractors, stock licenses, software, travel) are reimbursable at cost plus 10% administrative fee
- Expenses over $1,000 require prior written approval from Client
- Reimbursement invoiced separately with receipts attached

**Scope Adjustments:**
- If Client requests work beyond SOW scope, Elevra provides a change order specifying additional fees and timeline impact
- Elevra is not obligated to proceed with change requests until signed change order is received

---

## 4. DELIVERABLES & ACCEPTANCE

**Deliverables** are outlined in each SOW and include specifications, formats, revision rounds, and delivery dates.

**Revision & Approval Process:**
- Elevra delivers work according to SOW timeline
- Client has five (5) business days to review and provide feedback
- Two (2) rounds of revisions are included per deliverable (unless SOW specifies otherwise)
- Additional revisions are billed at $150/hour
- Client approval is required in writing before work is considered final

**Acceptance:**
- Client acceptance of deliverables is confirmed by signed approval or written acknowledgment
- If Client does not object within seven (7) days of delivery, deliverables are deemed accepted
- Accepted deliverables are final; further changes are treated as new scope

**Delays:**
- Elevra will use commercially reasonable efforts to meet deadlines
- Client delays in providing feedback, approvals, or assets may extend timelines
- Elevra is not liable for delays caused by Client or third-party factors beyond Elevra's control

## 5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY & OWNERSHIP

**Elevra's IP:** All pre-existing tools, methodologies, templates, frameworks, and processes used by Elevra remain Elevra's exclusive property. Client may not reverse-engineer, replicate, or share Elevra's proprietary systems without written permission.

**Work Product Ownership:**
- Upon full payment, ownership of all custom creative work (design, copy, video, photography) transfers to Client
- Client receives an exclusive, irrevocable license to use the work product
- Elevra retains the right to display the work in case studies, portfolio, and marketing materials (with Client permission)

**Third-Party Content:**
- Client is responsible for obtaining licenses or permissions for third-party assets (stock photos, fonts, music) used in deliverables
- Elevra does not warrant that work product does not infringe third-party rights; Client assumes liability for final use

**Moral Rights:**
- Elevra waives all moral rights claims to work product, except for attribution in case studies and public portfolio display

## 6. CONFIDENTIALITY & NDA

Both parties agree to keep all confidential information exchanged during this engagement confidential. Confidential information includes Client's business strategy, financial data, customer lists, and proprietary methods; and Elevra's processes, client lists, and business practices.

**Exceptions to Confidentiality:**
- Information already in the public domain
- Information legally required to be disclosed by law or court order
- Information disclosed with prior written consent
- Information independently developed without reference to confidential material

**Duration:** Confidentiality obligations survive termination of this MSA for three (3) years.

**Separate NDA:** If Client requires a separate Non-Disclosure Agreement, both parties agree to execute Elevra's standard mutual NDA concurrently with this MSA.
