## 5. SUCCESS METRICS & OUTCOMES

### Brand System Goals

- ✓ Differentiated visual identity that commands premium positioning in luxury hospitality market
- ✓ Clear brand messaging that resonates with target audience (high-net-worth travelers, C-suite decision-makers)
- ✓ Scalable brand system adaptable to new properties and marketing channels
- ✓ Website achieving 95+ Lighthouse performance score and conversion optimization baseline

### Measurement Framework

**Post-Launch (Month 1):**
- Website performance: traffic, bounce rate, pages/session, average session duration
- Brand awareness: social media engagement, mentions, sentiment analysis
- Preliminary conversion: form submissions, contact requests, newsletter signups

**60 Days Post-Launch:**
- Website conversion rate: contact form submissions, call-throughs, email inquiries
- Brand perception shift: compare to pre-launch baseline (if baseline data collected)
- Team adoption: internal feedback on brand system usability and clarity
